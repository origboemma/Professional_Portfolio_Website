# 6. Website Requirements

## 6.1 Purpose

This document defines the functional and non-functional requirements for the Professional Portfolio Website. These requirements describe what the website must do, how it should behave, and the quality standards it must satisfy to achieve the project objectives.

---

# 6.2 Stakeholders

The website is intended to serve the following stakeholders:

### Primary Stakeholders

- Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo (Website Owner)
- Recruiters
- Hiring Managers
- Internship Coordinators
- Employers
- Clients
- Professional Collaborators

### Secondary Stakeholders

- Fellow Developers
- Academic Supervisors
- Students
- Business Professionals
- General Visitors

---

# 6.3 Functional Requirements

The website shall provide the following functionality.

## FR-01 Home Page

The website shall provide a professional landing page that immediately introduces Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo and clearly communicates his professional focus.

---

## FR-02 Navigation

The website shall provide a responsive navigation menu that allows visitors to move easily between all major sections.

---

## FR-03 About Section

The website shall present Emmanuel's background, professional journey, career goals, and areas of specialization.

---

## FR-04 Skills Section

The website shall display technical, analytical, business, and professional skills using an organized and visually appealing layout.

---

## FR-05 Featured Projects

The website shall showcase completed projects covering Information Systems, Data Analytics, Business Analysis, Analytics Engineering, Data Engineering, Software Engineering, and Digital Business Solutions.

---

## FR-06 Certifications

The website shall display professional certifications with issuing organizations and completion dates.

---

## FR-07 Education

The website shall present academic qualifications, current studies, and relevant educational achievements.

---

## FR-08 Professional Experience

The website shall highlight relevant work experience, leadership roles, internships, and significant professional achievements.

---

## FR-09 Resume

The website shall allow visitors to download Emmanuel's latest résumé.

---

## FR-10 Contact

The website shall provide convenient methods for contacting Emmanuel, including:

- Email
- LinkedIn
- GitHub
- Other approved professional platforms

---

## FR-11 Responsive Design

The website shall function correctly across desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

## FR-12 Accessibility

The website shall be usable by visitors with different accessibility needs by following recognised accessibility practices where appropriate.

---

# 6.4 Non-Functional Requirements

The website shall satisfy the following quality requirements.

---

## NFR-01 Performance

- Fast page loading.
- Optimized images.
- Efficient CSS and JavaScript.
- Smooth scrolling and animations.

---

## NFR-02 Reliability

The website shall operate consistently without broken pages, missing assets, or navigation failures.

---

## NFR-03 Maintainability

The project structure shall support future expansion with minimal restructuring.

---

## NFR-04 Usability

Visitors shall understand the website structure without requiring instructions.

---

## NFR-05 Compatibility

The website shall function correctly on modern versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

---

## NFR-06 Security

The website shall avoid unnecessary third-party dependencies and follow secure front-end development practices.

---

## NFR-07 Scalability

The architecture shall support adding future projects, certifications, skills, and website sections without redesigning the entire website.

---

# 6.5 Content Requirements

The website shall include professionally written content for:

- Home
- About
- Professional Focus
- Skills
- Featured Projects
- Certifications
- Education
- Professional Experience
- Contact
- Footer

Every section shall communicate professionalism, clarity, and consistency.

---

# 6.6 Visual Requirements

The website shall provide:

- Premium visual appearance
- Modern typography
- Consistent spacing
- Professional colour palette
- High-quality icons
- Responsive images
- Elegant animations
- Excellent readability

---

# 6.7 Deployment Requirements

The completed website shall:

- Be hosted using GitHub Pages.
- Be publicly accessible.
- Connect to the GitHub repository.
- Support future custom domain integration.
- Be easy to maintain and update.

---

# 6.8 Requirements Summary

The Professional Portfolio Website shall combine excellent functionality, modern design, technical quality, usability, maintainability, and scalability to create a professional online presence that accurately represents Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo's skills, experience, projects, and long-term career aspirations.
