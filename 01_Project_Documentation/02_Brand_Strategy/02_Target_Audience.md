# 2. Target Audience

## 2.1 Purpose

This document identifies the intended audiences of the Professional Portfolio Website. Understanding the needs, expectations, and interests of each audience ensures that the website communicates effectively, presents relevant information, and supports Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo's long-term professional and career objectives.

---

# 2.2 Primary Target Audience

The primary audience consists of individuals and organisations that may directly influence Emmanuel's career opportunities.

These include:

- Recruiters
- Hiring Managers
- Human Resource Professionals
- Internship Coordinators
- Technical Team Leads
- Business Managers
- Consulting Firms
- Technology Companies

### Their Objectives

These visitors are primarily interested in:

- Professional credibility
- Technical competence
- Practical project experience
- Problem-solving ability
- Communication skills
- Evidence of continuous learning
- Professional documentation
- Portfolio quality

---

# 2.3 Secondary Target Audience

The secondary audience includes professionals who may collaborate with Emmanuel or learn from his work.

These include:

- Business Analysts
- Data Analysts
- Analytics Engineers
- Data Engineers
- Software Engineers
- Information Systems Professionals
- Researchers
- Academic Supervisors
- Students
- Technology Communities

### Their Objectives

These visitors are interested in:

- Technical approaches
- Project methodologies
- Documentation standards
- Software engineering practices
- Data engineering workflows
- Analytics engineering concepts
- Business analysis techniques
- Knowledge sharing

---

# 2.4 Future Business Audience

As Emmanuel's career develops, the website will also support business and consulting opportunities.

Potential visitors include:

- Business Owners
- Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
- Corporate Organisations
- Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs)
- Government Agencies
- Educational Institutions
- Startups
- Entrepreneurs

### Their Objectives

These visitors seek:

- Technology consulting
- Business process improvement
- Information systems solutions
- Data-driven decision support
- Digital transformation guidance
- Operational efficiency improvements

---

# 2.5 Educational Audience

The website is also intended to inspire and support learners.

This audience includes:

- University Students
- Internship Participants
- Early-Career Professionals
- Career Changers
- Self-Learners

They are expected to benefit from:

- Project examples
- Learning pathways
- Professional documentation
- Career development insights
- Best practices
- Technology recommendations

---

# 2.6 Audience Expectations

Regardless of background, visitors should expect the website to provide:

- Accurate information
- Professional presentation
- Well-documented projects
- Easy navigation
- Clear communication
- Modern design
- Practical evidence of skills
- Authentic professional achievements

---

# 2.7 Audience Experience Goals

Every visitor should leave the website with the following impressions:

- Emmanuel is a credible technology professional.
- Emmanuel understands both business and technology.
- Emmanuel applies structured problem-solving approaches.
- Emmanuel produces professional-quality work.
- Emmanuel demonstrates continuous learning and growth.
- Emmanuel is capable of contributing to real-world projects.

---

# 2.8 Audience Summary

The Professional Portfolio Website is designed to serve multiple audiences while maintaining a consistent professional identity. Whether the visitor is a recruiter, employer, client, collaborator, student, or business leader, the website will present relevant information that demonstrates Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo's expertise in **Information Systems, Business Analysis, Data Analytics, Analytics Engineering, Data Engineering, Software Engineering, and Digital Business Solutions**.
