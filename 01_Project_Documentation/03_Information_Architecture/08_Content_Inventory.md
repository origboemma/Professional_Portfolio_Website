# 8. Content Inventory

## 8.1 Purpose

This document identifies every content asset required to complete the Professional Portfolio Website.

The inventory serves as a planning checklist for writing, design, photography, screenshots, graphics, downloadable resources, and supporting materials.

Maintaining a complete content inventory ensures consistency, reduces duplication, and supports efficient website development.
