# 4. Page Hierarchy

## 4.1 Purpose

This document defines the relative importance of every page within the Professional Portfolio Website.

The hierarchy determines how information is prioritised, how visitors progress through the website, and how the overall user experience supports Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo's professional goals.

The objective is to present information in an order that naturally builds understanding, credibility, and trust.

---

# 4.2 Hierarchy Philosophy

The website follows a progressive hierarchy.

Visitors should gradually move from:

**Introduction → Understanding → Evidence → Validation → Engagement**

Rather than overwhelming visitors with large amounts of information immediately, the hierarchy reveals information logically as interest increases.

---

# 4.3 Primary Level Pages

These pages represent the highest level of importance.

## Home

Purpose:

Introduce Emmanuel's professional identity and provide an executive overview of the website.

---

## Projects

Purpose:

Showcase practical work and demonstrate the ability to solve real business problems.

Projects are considered the strongest evidence of professional capability.

---

## Contact

Purpose:

Provide clear opportunities for employers, recruiters, collaborators, and clients to connect.

---

# 4.4 Secondary Level Pages

These pages provide supporting information that strengthens credibility.

- About
- Professional Journey
- Skills & Technologies
- Certifications
- Experience
- Resume

These pages expand upon the summaries introduced on the homepage.

---

# 4.5 Tertiary Level Pages

These pages support the overall experience but are not central to the visitor journey.

Examples include:

- 404 Page
- Thank You Page
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use
- Search Results
- Project Archive (future)

---

# 4.6 Project Hierarchy

Within the Projects section, flagship projects should receive greater prominence.

Initial flagship projects include:

1. PrimeMart Retail Sales Operations Reporting System
2. Banking Operations Intelligence & Risk Reporting System
3. Sales Revenue Analysis Dashboard
4. Customer Segmentation Analysis
5. Web Scraping Project

As the portfolio grows, new flagship projects may be added.

---

# 4.7 Homepage Content Hierarchy

The homepage should present content in the following order:

1. Hero Section
2. Professional Introduction
3. Featured Projects
4. Skills & Technologies
5. Certifications
6. Professional Journey Preview
7. Experience Highlights
8. Contact Call-to-Action
9. Footer

This sequence introduces Emmanuel, demonstrates capability, establishes credibility, and encourages engagement.

---

# 4.8 Visual Hierarchy

Every page should establish a clear visual hierarchy using:

- Typography
- Colour
- Spacing
- Imagery
- Cards
- Buttons
- Icons
- Section headings

The most important information should receive the greatest visual emphasis.

---

# 4.9 Information Priority

Content should be prioritised according to visitor needs.

Highest priority:

- Professional value
- Business impact
- Featured projects
- Practical achievements

Supporting priority:

- Technical skills
- Certifications
- Career journey

Reference information:

- Policies
- Legal notices
- Supporting pages

# 4.11 Page Hierarchy Summary

The Professional Portfolio Website follows a carefully structured hierarchy that presents information in a logical progression from introduction to engagement. By prioritising professional value, practical projects, and clear communication, the hierarchy ensures that visitors quickly understand Emmanuel Chukwuka Origbo's expertise and are encouraged to explore further.
